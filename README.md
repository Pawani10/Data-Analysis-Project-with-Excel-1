# Vrinda Store Annual Sales Analysis 
## Project Objective
To analyze Vrinda Store's 2022 sales data and uncover actionable insights around revenue trends, customer demographics, order fulfillment, and channel performance — with the goal of helping the store identify its most valuable customer segments and optimize sales strategy for the following year.
## Dataset used
<a href="https://github.com/Pawani10/Data-Analysis-Project-with-Excel-1/blob/main/Vrinda%20Store%20Data%20Analysis%20(1).xlsx"> Raw Dataset </a>
## KPIs
- Total Revenue & Monthly Sales Trend
- Order Volume by Month
- Revenue Split by Gender
- Order Status Distribution (Delivered, Cancelled, Returned, Refunded)
- Top 5 States by Revenue
- Sales Channel Contribution (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, etc.)
- Customer Demographics by Age Group and Gender
